Application Requirements

Application forms can be handpicked from the Academy or downloaded from the Academy website. Applicants can also apply directly through the website. Duly filled application forms should be addressed to the Admissions Officer or hand-delivered to the Academy.

Read More

1. Application forms should be accompanied by;

a) For Kenyan Students:

  • Copies of the relevant academic and professional certificates or result slips
  • 2 passport size photographs
  • National ID/ or passport
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Completed ‘’fee payment options form’’
  • Certificate of Good Conduct from CID
  • Copy of food Handlers Certificate
  • Confirmation of Application fee payment (i.e. KES.1500.00 – non-refundable)
Read More

b) For International students;

  • Copies of the relevant academic and professional certificates or result slips
  • Copy of Certificate(s)/ Result slips of High School
  • Copies of Higher Education (Certificates, Diploma, Degree) if applicable
  • 4 Color Passport Photos
  • Copy of Passport – Bio Data Page
  • Copy of E-Visa (If already in Kenya)
  • Passport Copy of Parent / Guardian
  • Parents / Sponsor Financial Commitment Letter
  • Proof of Funds - This applies to SELF-SPONSORED STUDENTS (Attach copies of signed and stamped bank statements for the last 3 months)
  • Completed “Fee Payment Options Form”
  • Police Clearance from Country of Origin
  • Confirmation of Application Fee Payment – (i.e. KES. 1,500.00 - non-refundable)
  • Confirmation of Health Insurance Coverage Comprehensive cover
  • Copy of Student Pass & Foreign National ID card

2. English proficiency

3. Medical fitness

Some courses require students to be medically fit. Applicants with known long term medical conditions are encouraged to divulge this information.

Student Admission;

Applicants who have met the above entry requirements will be issued with an admission letter detailing the program into which they have been admitted and the fee payable.
