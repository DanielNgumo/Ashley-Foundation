Certificate in Professional Cookery

This is a beginners ‘course designed to introduce learners to essential kitchen skills in preparation for employment, with learning outcomes designed to develop practical cookery skills. Students are assessed on their practical cooking and kitchen skills rather than theoretical knowledge. Through the course, learners will develop their understanding of the core skills required in a professional kitchen; improving on a number of techniques, including the ability to handle kitchen equipment, prepare food and finish or store the products they create.

Course content:
  • Professional kitchen operations and safety techniques.
  • Prepare, cook and finish basic stocks, soups and sauces.
  • Prepare, cook and finish Vegetable, Rice and pasta dishes.
  • Prepare, cook and finish Basic Meats, Poultry and Offal dishes.
  • Prepare, cook and finish Basic Fresh and salty Fish dishes.

Certificate in Baking and Pastry

This course specializes in many aspects of pastry and baking; from dough kneading, cakes, bread making, Biscuits, chocolate work, to filling/piping and much more. The aim of this specialist course is to introduce learners to the core skills needed to work in a professional pastry kitchen or a commercial bakery.

Course content:
  • Professional kitchen operations and safety techniques.
  • Ingredients to pastry.
  • Bread and Dough products.
  • Pastries.
  • Cakes, Sponges and Biscuits.
  • Finishing and presentation skills.

Certificate in Cake Making and Decoration

This course is designed to introduce learners to the art of cake making and decoration. Students will learn the basics of mixing, shaping and baking for several cakes. The trainees will be introduced to various decoration techniques in a well-equipped modern kitchen. The course entails classroom instructions and practical lessons.

Course content:
  • Professional kitchen operations and safety techniques.
  • Introduction to cakes: Marble, Chocolate, Sponge, Company Logos.
  • Methods: Rubbing-in, Creaming, Whisking, Melted mixtures and combination methods
  • Fondant Making.
  • Icing, Piping and Glaces.
  • Styles in Cake decoration.
  • Finishing and presentation skills.
