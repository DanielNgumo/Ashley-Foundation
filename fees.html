Fees Payment and Academic Calenders

Prospective students and their guardians can access the fee structures for the respective courses by downloading the  HCA brochure (here) or contacting the admissions office. Fees is payable at the beginning of every term. Students who demonstrate the inability to pay the full amount may choose to pay fees in three monthly installments by the 5th day of every Month.

Besides tuition fee payable per term, other charges are;

No. Description Amount(Kes) Payment Mode
1 Admission fees 1,500.00 Once
2 Caution Money 1,000.00 Once; refundable if no damage caused by condition of negligence
3 Uniforms; Chefs Jacket, Apron & Neckerchief 2,700.00 Once
4 Academy Student ID 500.00 Once

Fees can be paid either through the Academy bank account or by means of an MPESA paybill.
